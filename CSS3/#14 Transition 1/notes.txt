> transition bisa dipake buat ngubah nilai dari property secara halus
> transition: property durasi fungsi delay
> durasi: s || ms

animateable properties:
> background-color
> background-position
> background—size
> border
> color
> filter
> font—size
> line-height
> top left bottom right
> margin padding
> width height
> opacity
> transform
> word-spacing
> letter-spacing
> etc

non-animateable properties:
> font-family
> display
> position
> etc
