> transition bisa dipake buat ngubah nilai dari property secara halus
> transition: property durasi fungsi delay
> durasi: s || ms

> property punya nilai default all
> bisa milih property mana aja yg mau dijalanin di transition
> animateable properties:
    > background-color
    > background-position
    > background—size
    > border
    > color
    > filter
    > font—size
    > line-height
    > top left bottom right
    > margin padding
    > width height
    > opacity
    > transform
    > word-spacing
    > letter-spacing
    > etc

non-animateable properties:
    > font-family
    > display
    > position
    > etc
